Instructions for use

Your UniLED light comes with 3 switch positions: DC, OFF, AC

DC Position

The bulb uses battery power, and works as an LED torch. Extend the handle to ensure a comfortable grip.

OFF Position

The bulb is switched off. Charging, will occur in this position.

AC Position (Wall Switch Control Mode)

The bulb responds to on-and-off triggering of your existing source switch (wall switch or lamp switch) irrespective of whether the mains power has been disconnected, as during load-shedding or deliberate intervention for power-saving purposes.
